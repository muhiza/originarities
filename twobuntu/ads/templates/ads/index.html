Advertising on 2buntu

Do you sell an application or service that you think would appeal to Ubuntu users? We'd love to have you advertise your product here. Your ad will be seen by our many visitors, drawing traffic and interest to your product.

What We Offer

We currently offer advertising exclusively on our home page. Advertisers pay a fixed fee for displaying an ad for a fixed period of time. We are very flexible in regards to campaign duration, so please contact us if you have any questions.

What We Accept

Although we welcome a diverse range of products, our policy is to limit advertising to products that relate to Ubuntu or at least Linux. We do this because we want to ensure that our visitors see only relevant ads - which makes both the advertiser and customer happy.

We also want to avoid displaying offensive or suggestive ads on our website. We reserve the right to refuse an ad that we deem is inappropriate for our visitors.
