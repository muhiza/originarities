About Us

ASI-D is a non-profit group of bloggers from around the world. We practice a style of reporting that's off the beaten path and designed for human beings.

Africa Smart Investments – Distribution ( ASI-D) offers innovative products combining high technology with affordable prices. We have won the trust of domestic and international markets because with technical assistance guaranteed at various points of the country, the user experiences high technology and innovation from the moment when the product is turned on. This translates our commitment with quality, now at hand!

Development Facts

ASI-D is written in Python and uses the Django web framework. The user interface uses Bootstrap 3.

Our articles are written in Markdown and stored in a PostgreSQL database. The Markdown is rendered to HTML and cached with Redis.
