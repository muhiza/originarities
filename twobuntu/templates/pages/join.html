Getting Started

The workflow for submitting an content looks something like this:

  • You create a new content by clicking your username in the upper-right corner and clicking "New Content".
  • The content editor will appear, providing you with space for entering the title, body, and category of the article/content You can also upload the files (.doc, .xls, .pdf, .ppt, .html Etc...).
